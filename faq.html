General Info.

Smart City is a technologically modern urban area that uses different types of electronic methods and sensors to collect specific data.

Information gained from that data is used to manage assets, resources and services efficiently; in return, that data is used to improve operations across the city.

Hence there is a need for developing a sustainable solution. The Smart Cities Mission was launched on June 25, 2015, with the key objective of promoting cities to provide core infrastructure, clean and sustainable environment and give a decent quality of life to their citizens through the application of ‘smart solutions’.

Contributing towards the Smart City Mission involves engaging in various activities that aim to enhance the quality of life, promote sustainability, and improve overall efficiency in urban areas.

About Urban Development

Urban development refers to the process of creating, planning, and implementing changes in the physical, social, and economic aspects of a city or urban area.

It encompasses a wide range of activities and interventions aimed at improving the overall quality of life for residents, promoting sustainability, and ensuring the efficient functioning of urban areas.
